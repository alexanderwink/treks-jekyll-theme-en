Security

Treks uses security technology that is similar to those of international banks. All data is encrypted and stored with advanced technology.

Certified Security

All communication between Treks and your web browser is encrypted using SSL. Treks is verified and secured by Verisign, world's leading provider of Internet trust services for authentication.

Separated Storage

Your data is separated from other customers. Even down to disk persistence. This way we can ensure you that your data never get into the wrong hands.

High Reliability

Treks is a cloud servive and runs on servers in the US and in Europe. Availability is above 99.95%. The service can be scaled up in times of high load.

Backup

Your data is safe with us. Multiple times every day a backup is made and stored off-site. All backups are encrypted.
