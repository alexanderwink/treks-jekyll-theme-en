Features

Regardless if you're a co-worker, reporting your hours on a daily basis, or if you're a consultant manager, compiling hours for your co-workers every month, Treks is the tool for you.

Registration

Time to work

As a co-worker you would want to spend as little time as possible reporting your times. Therefore a lot of effort has been put into the registration forms to be as intuitive and speedy as possible.

Reporting

Overview and Drill Down

With the reports available in Treks you can quickly get an overview of reported times. You can view times from a co-worker or customer perspective. We calculate revenue and cost automatically.

Invoicing and salaries

Integration with invoicing and salary software

Transfer time and salary data from Treks to your salary software and save time on administration. Invoices can be sent directly from within Treks or invoice data can be transfered to your invoicing software.

Gamification

Business and pleasure

Treks creates engagement when reporting times by introducing competitive tasks and achievements.
