Slack Integration

By integrating Treks and Slack you can take advantage of some extra features for your team. The Slack app is a companion to our service. With our app you can enter and edit your daily hours from within Slack. You can also lock the period and receive reminders when you've forgotten to do so.

So how does it work? Well, every day of work the bot will ask you at the end of the day what you've been working on. In most cases it will first ask you for the amount of hours, simple enter the number in the chat. The bot might then ask you for the name of the project you worked on, just type the project name in the chat. Finally the bot will confirm that the hours have been saved and show a small summary of the times entered for that day.

We're constantly adding more features to our Slack app. Watch this page for more information.

Chat commands

You can order the bot around if you want to. Here are a list of command you can send to the bot and it will perform and respond to your actions.

help
Displays usage information. Basically it's the same as you're reading here.
log
Log today's hours. The bot will ask you for hours and project.
log <yymmdd|mmdd|dd> <hours> <project>
Log hours on the specific date and project.
log <hours> <project>
Log today's hours on a specific project.
log <hours>
Log today's hours. The bot will ask you for the project.
lock
Lock the period.
unlock
Unlock the period.
list | list projects
List all available projects available to you for logging hours.
cancel
Stop what you're doing.
